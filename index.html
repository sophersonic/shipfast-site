FAQ

Common questions

What tech stack do you use?

We use clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for most projects. For more complex apps, we use React. No WordPress, no page builders, no bloated frameworks. Just fast, maintainable code.

How does the 48-hour delivery work?

Once you submit your brief and payment, the timer starts. We build your site and deliver it within 48 hours. Complex Pro-tier projects may take slightly longer, but we'll let you know upfront.

What if I need changes after delivery?

One round of revisions is included with every tier. Need more? We're happy to make additional changes at a fair hourly rate. Just reach out.

Who owns the code?

You do. 100%. After delivery, the source code is entirely yours. Host it anywhere, modify it freely, hire someone else to extend it. No lock-in, no licensing restrictions.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee in accordance with EU consumer protection law. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of delivery for a full refund.

Can I see examples of your work?

Absolutely. Email us at hello@shipfast.site and we'll send you relevant examples based on the type of project you're planning. Every project is custom, so we match references to your needs.
